I am a senior undergraduate at [[https://cs.stanford.edu][Stanford University]] majoring in computer science (AI track). I've been a member of Professor [[https://dorsa.fyi][Dorsa Sadigh]]'s [[https://iliad.stanford.edu/][ILIAD]] lab since my sophomore year.

I am interested in creating AI agents that can interact with their environment and safely work alongside humans and other autonomous agents. My research broadly spans three subfields of computer science:

\bullet Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning: Enabling independently trained agents to cooperate on a common task and form conventions.

\bullet Vision: Capturing meaningful information about an agent's environment from sensors.

\bullet Graphics: Simulating environments while balancing speed and realism.
